As a software engineer, delays and issues can be prevented if we follow a strict methodology. We are all professionals so it's a must to follow certain rules that are implemented to prevent such an issue. 
Proper communication with the team and we should note the changes always to monitor the progress. There are so many types of project management that can be used by the team to monitor the changes or progress and also anticipate the time. 
In the project, there is a beginning and end and also a goal with the proper discipline and cooperation of the team even if we encounter delays and problems along the way the project will be done on time.
